Weekly Workout & Meal Plan

Monday

Workout: Gym Session (Full-Body, 1 hour)

Warm-up, compound movements, arm focus, cool-down.

Meals: Muesli with Milk, Egg & Bacon Sandwich, Grilled Chicken with Sweet Potato Mash


Tuesday

Workout: Office Day: Cardio & Mobility

30–45 minute run or HIIT/home circuit, plus 10–15 minutes stretching/yoga.

Meals: Muesli with Milk, Egg & Bacon Sandwich, Grilled Chicken with Sweet Potato Mash


Wednesday

Workout: Office Day: Active Recovery

Gentle activity (brisk walk) plus 15–20 minutes of stretching/yoga.

Meals: Muesli with Milk, Egg & Bacon Sandwich, Grilled Chicken with Sweet Potato Mash


Thursday

Workout: Gym Session (Full-Body, 1 hour)

Dynamic warm-up, compound movements, arm focus, cool-down.

Meals: Muesli with Milk, Egg & Bacon Sandwich, Grilled Chicken with Sweet Potato Mash


Friday

Workout: Gym Session (Full-Body, 1 hour)

Light cardio warm-up, compound movements, arm focus, cool-down.

Meals: Muesli with Milk, Egg & Bacon Sandwich, Grilled Chicken with Sweet Potato Mash


Saturday

Workout: Cardio & Optional Home Circuit

30–45 minute run or brisk walk; optional bodyweight circuit; full-body stretching.

Meals: Muesli with Milk, Egg & Bacon Sandwich, Grilled Chicken with Sweet Potato Mash


Sunday

Workout: Rest or Light Recovery

Complete rest or gentle activity (leisurely walk, restorative yoga).

Meals: Muesli with Milk, Egg & Bacon Sandwich, Grilled Chicken with Sweet Potato Mash

Monday – Full Breakdown

Workout:

Gym Session (Push Day)

  • Warm-up: 5 minutes of light cardio (treadmill or cycle)
  • Chest, Shoulders, Triceps + Quads/Calves
    • Bench Press – 4×8
    • Overhead Press – 3×10
    • Dumbbell Lateral Raises – 3×12
    • Tricep Dips or Pushdowns – 3×12
    • Goblet Squats or Front Squats – 3×10
    • Standing Calf Raises – 3×15

    • Meals:

    Breakfast: Muesli with Milk

    Lunch: Egg & Bacon Sandwich

    Dinner: Grilled Chicken with Sweet Potato Mash

Tuesday – Full Breakdown

Workout:

Office Day: Cardio & Mobility

  • Option 1: Brisk 30–45 minute run (before or after work)
  • Option 2: Home-based HIIT or bodyweight circuit (20–30 minutes)
  • Additional: 10–15 minute stretching or yoga routine

Meals:

Breakfast: Muesli with Milk

Lunch: Egg & Bacon Sandwich

Dinner: Grilled Chicken with Sweet Potato Mash

Wednesday – Full Breakdown

Workout:

Office Day: Active Recovery

  • Focus: Gentle activity such as a brisk walk
  • Additional: 15–20 minutes of stretching or yoga targeting major muscle groups

Meals:

Breakfast: Muesli with Milk

Lunch: Egg & Bacon Sandwich

Dinner: Grilled Chicken with Sweet Potato Mash

Thursday – Full Breakdown

Workout:

Gym Session (Pull Day)

  • Warm-up: 5 minutes of dynamic movements
  • Back, Biceps, Glutes/Hamstrings:
    • Pull-Ups or Lat Pulldown – 3×10
    • Dumbbell or Barbell Rows – 3×10
    • Seated Cable Rows – 3×12
    • Romanian Deadlifts – 3×10 (hits glutes & hamstrings)
    • Barbell or Dumbbell Bicep Curls – 3×10
    • Hammer Curls – 3×12
    • Optional: Face Pulls – 3×15
  • Cool-down: 5 minutes of stretching

Meals:

Breakfast: Muesli with Milk

Lunch: Egg & Bacon Sandwich

Dinner: Grilled Chicken with Sweet Potato Mash

×

Friday – Full Breakdown

Workout:

Upper Body Finisher + Unilateral Legs + Core

  • Warm-up: 5 minutes of light cardio
  • Exercises:
    • Incline Dumbbell Press – 3×10
    • Arnold Press – 3×10
    • Concentration or Preacher Curls – 3×12
    • Overhead Tricep Extensions – 3×12
    • Bulgarian Split Squats – 3×8 each leg (great for glutes/balance)
    • Plank – 3×1 min
    • Leg Raises or Cable Crunches – 3×15
  • Cool-down: 5 minutes of stretching

Meals:

Breakfast: Muesli with Milk

Lunch: Egg & Bacon Sandwich

Dinner: Grilled Chicken with Sweet Potato Mash

×

Saturday – Full Breakdown

Workout:

Cardio & Optional Home Circuit

  • Cardio: 30–45 minute run or mix of running and brisk walking
  • Optional: Short bodyweight circuit (e.g., push-ups, planks, squats)
  • Cool-down: Full-body stretching

Meals:

Breakfast: Muesli with Milk

Lunch: Egg & Bacon Sandwich

Dinner: Grilled Chicken with Sweet Potato Mash

×

Sunday – Full Breakdown

Workout:

Rest or Light Recovery

  • Complete rest or gentle activity (leisurely walk, restorative yoga)
